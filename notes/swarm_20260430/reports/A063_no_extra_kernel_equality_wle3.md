must be the source presentation map

```tex

\widehat{\mathfrak F}(\mathscr B_{\Delta_5})_{\le W}
```

not a target-basis identification. It is determined by

```tex

e_i\mapsto Q_{\alpha_i}\tilde e_i^X,\qquad
f_i\mapsto Q_{-\alpha_i}\tilde f_i^X,
```

for simple roots `\alpha_i\in W`, then extended by the reduced source
bracket `QB^X`. The finite domain is the triangular projection to words
with letters in `W\cup(-W)\cup\{0\}`; cancellation-only words are
deferred to exact completion. Anchors: `main.tex` near lines 12911 and
15314.

## Kernel Formula

The required equality is

```tex
\ker \pi_{R,W}
 =
\bigl(\overline{J_{\mathrm{BK}}
  +\operatorname{Rad}_{\mathrm{GN}}}\bigr)_{\le W},
```

equivalently the finite projection of

```tex

  =\overline{J_{\mathrm{BK}}+\operatorname{Rad}_{\mathrm{GN}}}.
```

This is not NO7. NO7 only legitimizes radical descent. Kernel equality
is the no-extra-relations theorem itself. Anchors: `main.tex` near lines
12714, 12920, and 15321.
